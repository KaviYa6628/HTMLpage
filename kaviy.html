kaviya

Kaviya Maran is the CEO of Sunrisers Hyderabad, an Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise. She is also the co-owner of the team. Born in Chennai on August 6, 1992, she is the daughter of Kalanithi Maran, chairman of Sun Group, a major media conglomerate. Beyond cricket, she is actively involved in the business operations of Sun TV Network and the Sun Group.
